Sad

We want you to recall a situation that made you sad.
Our goal is for you to experience the same emotions as you did then, so choose a situation that you have a clear memory of.
Examples of such memories could be the loss of someone you love, the feeling of being left alone etc.
Write down an as vivid description of the situation as possible. You may include what happend, what do you see and hear and how did that make you feel.

Note! No one is going to read what you write so there is no need to hold back.
