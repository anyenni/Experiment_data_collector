Relaxed

We want you to recall the situation where you feel the most relaxed.
Perhaps it is to spend a nice summer day lying in the sun, or curling up in front of the fireplace with a cup of hot chocolate and a good book.
Our goal is for you to experience the same emotions as you did then, so choose a situation that you are able to describe well.
Write down an as vivid description of the situation as possible. You may include what happend, what do you see and hear and how did that make you feel.

Note! No one is going to read what you write so there is no need to hold back.
