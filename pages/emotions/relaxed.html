Relaxed

We want you to recall a typical situation where you feel relaxed.

Perhaps it is spending a nice summer day lying in the sun, or curling up in front of the fireplace with a cup of hot chocolate and a good book.

Our goal is for you to experience the same emotions as you did then, so choose a situation that you are able to describe well.

Write down an as vivid description of the situation as possible. You may include what happend, what do you see and hear and how did that make you feel.

You can write as long as you feel, but we will stop you after 10 minutes.

No one is going to read what you write. So do not let that hold you back!

Please do not press "Continue" until you are finished with the task!
