Afraid

Please describe in detail the one situation that made you the most afraid you have been in your life, and describe it such that a person reading the description would become afraid.

Our goal is for you to experience the same emotions as you did then, so choose a situation with strong feelings that you have a clear memory of.

You may include what you see, hear and feel. The goal is for you to experience the same emotions as you did then, so don't hold back!

You can write as long as you feel, but we will stop you after 10 minutes.

Note that one is going to read what you write.

Please do not press "Continue" until you are finished with the task!
